About Me

I am a 3rd year math PhD student at NYU working with Dr. Mike O'Neil on fast integral equation solvers for wave scattering problems with applications in acoustic and electromagnetic phenomena.

I primarily develop algorithms in Julia and many of my projects have an FFT flavor.
